About me

I am from Ghana, where my favorite food is fufu and groundnut soup. Back home, I work as a pharmacist, but I am currently studying computer programming at Seneca Polytechnic in Canada. I have a diverse set of skills including React, Express, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, C++, HTML, JavaScript, and React Native. Additionally, I am passionate about technology and enjoy exploring new tools and frameworks to enhance my development capabilities.

This semester, I am working on my WEB322 assignment, a project built using Express, JavaScript, HTML, and styled with Tailwind CSS and DaisyUI. This project will span the entire semester, with continuous development and improvements. It aims to showcase my ability to integrate different technologies and create a robust web application. I am excited about this opportunity to apply my knowledge in a practical setting and to further expand my programming skills.
